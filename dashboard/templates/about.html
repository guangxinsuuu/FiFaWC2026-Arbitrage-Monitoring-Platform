About / Support

WC2026 Opportunity Engine is a free, community-supported project focused on real-time bookmaker monitoring and opportunity discovery.

The platform tracks odds across multiple sportsbooks and surfaces arbitrage opportunities, middle betting opportunities, market divergence, and pricing inefficiencies in real time.

Running live odds infrastructure is not free. Donations help cover API usage, cloud hosting, data storage, and future development.

If the platform saves you time, teaches you something new, or helps you discover opportunities you would have otherwise missed, consider supporting the project.
